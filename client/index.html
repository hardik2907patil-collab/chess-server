🔮 Checkmate Legends Guide

👑

The Objective

The goal of chess is to "checkmate" your opponent's king. This happens when their king is under attack and cannot escape capture.

  • White always moves first.
  • Players take turns moving one piece per turn.
  • Pieces capture by landing on a square occupied by an opponent's piece.
♞

How Pieces Move

Every piece has its unique strength and movement pattern:

  • Pawn (♟): Moves forward 1 square (or 2 on its first move). Captures diagonally.
  • Knight (♞): Moves in an L-shape (2 squares one direction, 1 square perpendicular). Can jump over other pieces!
  • Bishop (♝): Moves diagonally any number of open squares.
  • Rook (♜): Moves horizontally or vertically any number of open squares.
  • Queen (♛): The most powerful. Moves both diagonally and straight like a Bishop + Rook.
  • King (♚): Moves exactly 1 square in any direction.
🛡️

Winning Strategy

Master these core principles to become a legend:

  • Control the Center: Aim to position your pawns and knights in the middle four squares early on.
  • Develop Your Pieces: Don't just move pawns. Get your knights and bishops out early to prepare for battle.
  • Protect Your King (Castle): Move your king to safety by castling early. Castling moves the king two squares toward a rook, and the rook hops over it.
  • Think Ahead: Always ask yourself "Why did my opponent make that move?" before playing yours.
